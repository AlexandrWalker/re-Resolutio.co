Overview

Task

The challenge was to grow the school's revenue by describing their courses with copy and visuals that hook.

Solution

We decided to build an up-to-date landing page following the marketing strategy. Its user flow lets potential students navigate through relevant content easily, and the absence of complex graphics improves the site's loading speed.

Research and UI Design

In a competitive analysis, we noticed that webpages of other programming schools look pretty similar, and not all of them allow clients to find the information they need rapidly.


As for design, we found a flat style with simple but stylish graphics and animations the most suitable.

Result

As an upshot, we have a convenient, trendy, and fast-loading website, which guides the user through all sales funnel stages to purchase.
